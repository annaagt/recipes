
INGREDIENTS:
2 1/2 sticks unsalted butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 eggs
2 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp salt
2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

DIRECTIONS:
1. Preheat oven to 375F 
2. Beat butter and sugars until light and fluffy. Then add vanilla extract and eggs.
3. In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking soda and salt. Stir into butter-sugar mixture. 
Fold in chocolate chips.
4. Roll dough into one inch balls  
Bake cookies until lightly golden, about 10 minutes.
